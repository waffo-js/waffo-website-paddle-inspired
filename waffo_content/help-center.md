# Help Center

Find answers to common questions about Waffo. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

How do I get started with Waffo?

Getting started is simple! Sign up for an account, verify your business details, and you'll have access to our API documentation and sandbox environment within minutes.

What payment methods do you support?

We support 430+ payment methods including credit cards, digital wallets, bank transfers, and local payment methods across 50+ regions.

How long does it take to integrate Waffo?

With our simple API and comprehensive documentation, most integrations take just a few hours. Our developer support team is available 24/7 to help.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You can test Waffo with our free sandbox environment. Start processing real transactions and upgrade to a production account when you're ready.

What about transaction fees?

Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. Contact our sales team for a custom quote based on your transaction volume and requirements.

How secure is Waffo?

We use industry-leading security standards including PCI DSS Level 1 compliance, end-to-end encryption, and fraud detection to protect your data.
