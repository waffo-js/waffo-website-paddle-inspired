We gather statistical information related to your website usage and online interactions with our Platform, and we also permit third parties (including but not limited to Amazon Web Services, Facebook, and Google) to collect such data using "cookies" and similar technologies. This statistical information is anonymized and does not reveal your identity; it encompasses metrics like page visits, access timestamps, dates, and browser types. We collect this data for analysis and assessment purposes, aiding us in enhancing our Platform.

"Cookies" are a functionality within your internet browser that stores minimal data on your computer when you visit a website. They enable us to identify a specific device or browser, enabling us to tailor and customize content according to your preferences, thereby enhancing the usefulness of our services and Platform for you. "Cookies" do not reveal your identity unless you willingly provide us with personally identifiable information. You may have the option to control and remove "cookies" by choosing specific settings in your browser.

We may share your statistical data with our third-party partners in the following contexts:
