Here's a look at the AI tech startups driving innovation at the application-level by being super quick to ship the latest LLM model releases – allowing their customers to have access to the latest and greatest.

**For productivity enthusiasts:**[you.com](https://you.com/) has integrated Google’s Gemini 2.0 Flash into its platform, joining its existing lineup of language models like DeepSeek R1 and o3 Mini, the company announced in an email. The addition hopes to further enhance you's research and content creation capabilities for users looking for efficiency and speed in its flagship GPT-inspired productivity chat product.

**Sales and support:**[Lindy.ai](https://lindy.ai/), an agentic AI tool that allows automation across entire sales, marketing, and operations stacks, also expanded its model offerings with five new models, including Gemini 2.0 Flash and Claude Haiku 3.5. The update also introduces new context management actions, allowing users to add or clear task context, which can help [streamline workflows](https://www.lindy.ai/solutions/email) and improve task automation.

Similarly, [Chatsimple](https://chatsimple.ai/) has upgraded its AI agents with OpenAI's o3-mini model, enhancing the power and accuracy of its inbound sales and support solutions. The company noted that existing users of the o1-mini model have been automatically upgraded to benefit from these improvements.

**Huge (re)-launch on PH:** Lastly, agentic CX powerhouse [Chatbase](https://www.chatbase.co/blog/chatbase-on-product-hunt) launched its [new platform on Product Hunt](https://www.producthunt.com/posts/chatbase-5). The suite transitions from simple chatbots to smart AI agents capable of reasoning and taking actions. Built on OpenAI’s 4o models, Chatbase allows AI agents to perform tasks like changing subscriptions and booking appointments, integrating with [leading payment tools](https://www.paddle.com/billing/payments) and popular calendars, prioritizing interactive and efficient customer support for all.
