## What's this session about:

Ready to launch your Web2App strategy but not sure where to start?   
  
Join Nathan Hudson, CEO of Perceptycs and App Marketer of the Year 2024 and Andrew Davies, CMO at Paddle, for the second of this three-part series, as they guide you through proven implementation approaches, from choosing the right funnel type to selecting the best payment infrastructure for your needs.  
  
**This is an interactive practical workshop to help you grow and scale your app in 2025 so we will be asking for all registrants to participate, ask questions, and share their experiences throughout the sessions.**  


**What to expect:**

  * Get a practical checklist for launching your first [Web2App funnel](https://www.paddle.com/resources/mobile-app-monetization-guide), including technical requirements and resource planning.
  * Learn how to avoid common implementation pitfalls that can impact conversion rates.
  * Master the different types of Web2App funnels: from simple landing pages and buy buttons to quizzes, webinars, blog content, and complex onboarding flows.
