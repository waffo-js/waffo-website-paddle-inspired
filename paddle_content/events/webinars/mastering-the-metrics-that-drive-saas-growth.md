## What's this session about:

Your growth as a SaaS founder hinges on metrics. Master the data, and take your company to the next level.

Join Bill Kerr, Founder and CEO at Athyna, Writer at Open Source CEO and Paddle's Gavin Dove as they help you leverage data to find hidden growth opportunities, and tell compelling stories that galvanize your company into action.

**Why attend?**

  * **Setting up for success:** Identify your most important metrics and create dashboards with actionable insights for your business stage.
  * **Data-driven decision making:** Analyze unit economics, revenue streams, churn factors, and retention patterns to focus on high-impact opportunities.
  * **Effective storytelling:** Translate complex data into compelling narratives that resonate with your team, investors, and board.
  * **Turning metrics to growth:** Implement strategies to attract customers, monetize effectively, and drive retention.


Whether starting up or scaling, you'll gain tools and first-hand experience to make data-driven decisions and take your growth to the next level.
