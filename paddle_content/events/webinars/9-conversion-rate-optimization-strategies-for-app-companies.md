## What's this session about:

You don't need more traffic or downloads to make more money. You need to focus on visitors taking the desired action on your website or app. 

If you are an app company focused on growth, every interaction with a user is an opportunity to drive engagement, conversions, and long-term revenue.

Join Paddle's VP of Product Lucas Lovell and Steve Young, Founder of App Masters break down 9 key strategies to kickstart your conversion rate optimization.

From optimizing your home page to effectively monetizing your checkout, these strategies will drive growth in 2025.
