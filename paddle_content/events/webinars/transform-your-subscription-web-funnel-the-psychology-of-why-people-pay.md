## What's this session about:

Look beyond basic questionnaires or quizzes. 

The most successful Web2App funnels create a natural conversation that builds clear value and guides users toward an inevitable "yes!."

Join web2wave Founder, Igor Lyubimov and Paddle’s Gavin Dove as they dive into the psychology of high-converting subscription funnels that feel less like selling and more like helping. 

They will help you unlock revenue beyond the app store. 

**In this webinar, you'll discover:**

  * The hidden psychological triggers that transform browsers into buyers.
  * A proven three-part funnel framework.
  * How to identify and eliminate the specific friction points costing you conversions.
  * Value-building techniques that make your subscription feel like an obvious next step, not a hard sell.
  * Strategies will help you unlock more revenue beyond app stores with a Merchant of Record.
