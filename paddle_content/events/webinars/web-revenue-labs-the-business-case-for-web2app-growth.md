## What's this session about:

Whether you're a new app just starting out or an established player doing millions in revenue, [Web2App strategies](https://www.paddle.com/resources/web2app-funnel-fundamentals) can unlock significant growth. 

Join Nathan Hudson, CEO of Perceptycs and App Marketer of the Year 2024 and Andrew Davies, CMO at Paddle, for the first of our three-part series as they break down the benefits and approaches for apps at every stage - from reducing acquisition costs for early-stage apps to expanding into new markets for scaled companies.  
  
**This is an interactive practical workshop to help you grow and scale your app in 2025 so we will be asking for all registrants to participate, ask questions, and share their experiences throughout the sessions.**

[**Read the full playbook from the session here**](https://www.paddle.com/resources/web2app-funnel-fundamentals)

  
**What to expect:**

  * Early-stage apps: Learn how Web2App can improve your cash flow, reduce initial marketing costs, and help you test channels faster.
  * Growing apps: Discover how to use Web2App for faster experimentation, better marketing attribution, and reduced platform fees.
  * Scaled apps: See how Web2App enables audience expansion and significant savings at scale.
