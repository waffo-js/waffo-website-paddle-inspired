## What's this session about:

This virtual event will feature the pitches and product demos from top founders emerging from the latest 5-week cohort of our prestigious startup accelerator program: Paddle SaaS Launchpad.   
  
The program is designed to help ambitious software founders build, launch and scale their SaaS business ideas. 

**What to Expect:**

  * Captivating startup pitches: A shortlist of the top SaaS startups from the latest cohort will take the virtual stage to present their innovative products. The demo day winner will be awarded $10k in cash.
  * Expert judging panel: A panel of esteemed investors and prominent founders will evaluate the pitches, ask questions and offer live feedback.
  * Watch and vote: You can watch the pitches, ask your burning questions in the chat and vote for your favourite startup to win the “People’s Choice Award” at the end!
