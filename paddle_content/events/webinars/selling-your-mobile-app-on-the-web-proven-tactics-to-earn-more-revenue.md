## What's this session about:

Monetizing from the web and [selling outside the app store ](https://www.paddle.com/solutions/web-stores)gives you the freedom and opportunity to leverage a direct-to-consumer model. This approach allows you to reach a larger audience, enhance your marketing efforts, and experiment with various ways to monetize and grow your app.  
  
In this webinar, we'll cover everything you need to know about establishing a web-based revenue stream.  
  
You'll get:

  * A full breakdown of the three key mobile [app monetization strategies](https://www.paddle.com/resources/mobile-app-monetization-guide): Web2App, Web Store, and Hybrid.
  * Expert tips on choosing the right web strategy for your app, including a comparison of supporting tools.
  * Data-backed insights on pricing and payments to drive higher-value purchases and reduce churn.
