## What's this session about:

Leading subscription apps are growing fast with innovative product development and predictable revenue streams. But how do you scale with marketplace saturation, subscriber churn, and user monetization?

Join Paddle and Phil Carter, Founder of Elemental Growth as they discuss how to build and execute a playbook to accelerate the growth of your consumer subscription app.

Phil splits his framework into three core stages: value creation, value delivery, and value capture. Watch the on-demand webinar to explore each stage, understanding how the process can help level up your consumer subscription app growth in 2025.
