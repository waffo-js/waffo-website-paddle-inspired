Requesting Buyer Data Deletion

## **Can buyers request their own data deletion?**

Yes. Buyers can request deletion of their personal data directly using our form: [Request Data Deletion](https://preferences.paddle.com/form/deletion?locationCode=GB-ENG).

## **Can vendors request deletion on behalf of buyers?**

Yes. However, vendors should get in touch via [sellers@paddle.com](mailto:sellers@paddle.com) and provide the information below.

## **What information is required?**

  * Buyer’s first name
  * Buyer’s last name
  * Buyer’s email address
  * Optional comments (do not include sensitive information)


## **What happens after the request is submitted?**

  * Paddle processes the request and sends confirmation to the buyer.
  * Vendors should delete any buyer data they hold.


## **Will Paddle keep any data?**

Yes. Some transactional data is retained for 5 years to comply with legal requirements. All other data is deleted.
