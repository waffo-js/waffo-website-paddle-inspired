Which emails will customers receive on Paddle Billing?

By default, Paddle will email customers with their transaction receipts along with any instructions you have provided for products. The best way to preview the emails your customers will receive is to [test your checkout integration](https://www.paddle.com/help/start/set-up-paddle/how-do-i-test-my-checkout-integration) using the full purchase & refund method.

## Email Localization

Paddle automatically translates these emails into the 16 languages currently supported in the Checkout: English, French, Portuguese, German, Spanish, Russian, Japanese, Italian, Polish, Dutch, Arabic, Chinese (simplified), Korean, Swedish, Danish, and Norwegian. This means customers will receive all subscription and transaction emails in the language they checkout in, which can help decrease involuntary churn and boost conversions in the post-purchase journey.

# Enhanced Billing Notifications

To comply with increasing regulatory requirements and card scheme rules, weâ€™ve introduced Enhanced Billing Notifications. When required, these notifications will automatically inform buyers of key billing events, ensuring transparency and reducing the risk of chargebacks.

We have developed these notifications with input from our Design, Legal, and Compliance teams to deliver a targeted, nuanced solution, that ensures optimal performance while ensuring compliance.

  


## Free Trial End Reminder

All customers will receive an email notification at least 7 days before their free trial ends. Card scheme rules require this reminder.

For free trials that are 7 days or less, this Free Trial End Reminder will not be sent. Paddle will only send the [Subscription Confirmation email](#subscription-confirmation). 

**Subject Line:**_Your Company Name free trial is about to end_

![](https://cdn.kustomerhostedcontent.com/media/5b64d5e964987defc0b2a2de/62eacf709e676c07ae8d44b92358466c.png)

  


## Discount Period End Reminder

All customers will be notified via email at least 7 days before a discounted period concludes. Card scheme rules require this. 

**Subject Line:**_Your Company Name discount is about to end_

![](https://cdn.kustomerhostedcontent.com/media/5b64d5e964987defc0b2a2de/44856b2fc32b9f60160c634a9907ad9e.png)

## **Auto-Renewal Reminder**

We will only send auto-renewal reminder emails to customers in locations where it's required by law, and only for subscriptions of 6 months or longer (i.e. 180 days or longer). Customers will get this email notification either 7 days or 30 days before the auto-renewal.

**Subject Line:**_Your Company Name subscription is about to renew_

![](https://cdn.kustomerhostedcontent.com/media/5b64d5e964987defc0b2a2de/450009dba974f0de8e146a0a387b25a6.png)

We will only send auto-renewal emails in locations required by law. [See the list of locations here.](https://www.paddle.com/help/manage/your-customers/enhanced-billing-notifications-location-list)
