What currencies can I invoice in?

At this time, you can create invoices in US dollars, Euros, and British Pounds. We’re investigating other currencies based on demand; let us know via [sellers@paddle.com](mailto:sellers@paddle.com) if there are other currencies you would be interested in us supporting. Your customers will receive different wire transfer payment details based on their location and currency.
