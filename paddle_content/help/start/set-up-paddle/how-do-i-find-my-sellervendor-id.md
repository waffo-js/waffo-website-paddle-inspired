# How do I find my Seller/Vendor ID? - Help Center - Paddle

_Easily locate your Seller/Vendor ID from your Paddle dashboard_

Seller ID is referenced in Paddle Billing (PB), whereas Vendor ID is reference in Paddle Classic (PC).

To find your Seller ID in PB, follow these simple steps:

  1. Sign in to your Paddle dashboard at [vendors.paddle.com](https://login.paddle.com/)

  2. Click the **'...'** icon in the top-left corner, in the sidebar

  3. Your **Seller****ID** will be shown


Alternatively, in PC:

  1. Select **Developer Tools** then **Authentication** in the sidebar

  2. Your **Vendor ID** will be shown**  
**


Keep this ID safe—it’s required for API access and support queries.

### **Still can’t find it?**

Use the chat widget in the dashboard or email us at [sellers@paddle.com](mailto:sellers@paddle.com) from the email linked to your Paddle account so we can assist you directly.
