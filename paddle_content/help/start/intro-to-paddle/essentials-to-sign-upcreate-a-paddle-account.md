Essentials to sign up/create a Paddle account

You can [sign up for a Paddle account](https://www.paddle.com/get-started) and start selling immediately.

[Here](https://developer.paddle.com/build/overview) is a step-by-step guide to creating your first product. You will have to add your website domain to your list of [approved domains](https://www.paddle.com/help/start/account-verification/what-is-domain-verification) to launch the checkout successfully.

Remember to fill in your details and configure your setup in:

  * [Account Settings](https://vendors.paddle.com/account-settings)
  * [Currencies Settings](https://vendors.paddle.com/currencies-v2)
  * [Checkout Settings](https://vendors.paddle.com/checkout-settings)
  * [Payout Settings](https://vendors.paddle.com/payouts/settings)
  * [Sales Tax Settings](https://vendors.paddle.com/tax)


Please consult our [developer documentation](https://developer.paddle.com/) for more complex integrations.

We conduct periodic reviews of our sellers to ensure they comply with our [terms of use](https://www.paddle.com/legal/terms).
