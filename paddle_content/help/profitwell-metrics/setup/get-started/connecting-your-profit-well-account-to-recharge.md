Connecting your ProfitWell account to Recharge

Connecting your Recharge account to ProfitWell is super easy.

  1. Log into ProfitWell and navigate to the integrations tab![](https://files.helpdocs.io/gvtU4sx5eD/articles/jh2dhhg38m/1585699568218/image.png)
  2. Select Recharge![](https://files.helpdocs.io/gvtU4sx5eD/articles/tss9dhjfbt/1607394307129/image.png)
  3. Navigate over to Settings and enter in your URL.


![](https://cdn.kustomerhostedcontent.com/media/5b64d5e964987defc0b2a2de/51b0fbe24d1bfa186abc8b41abb0f12d.png)  
  


4\. Approve the integration in your ReCharge account.

5\. Wait for your data to ingest into ProfitWell. This process usually takes a couple hours.

  


Any questions? Reach out to **support@profitwell.com!**
