It gives you the freedom and opportunity to leverage a direct-to-consumer model where you can reach a bigger audience, enhance your marketing efforts, and experiment with different ways to monetize and grow your app.  


This guide covers everything you need to know about opening up a web-based revenue stream.   


You’ll get:

  * A full breakdown of the 3 key [app monetization strategies](https://www.paddle.com/resources/mobile-app-monetization-guide): Web2App, Web Store, Hybrid
  * Expert tips to nail the right web strategy for your app, including a comparison of the tools to support this. 
  * Data-backed insights on pricing and payments to drive higher-value purchases and minimize churn.
