# Procurement information

Paddle operates as a reseller and merchant of record. Paddle has a non-exclusive licence to resell software to buyers on behalf of software suppliers. Paddle is, therefore, the principal in the sales transaction between Paddle and the software buyer, and provides customer support for all order-related queries.

However, while buyers are acquiring the product from Paddle, the software developer remains present in the arrangement acting as the product’s licensor. In practice what this means is, in the same way that if you were to buy a computer through a retailer; purchase related queries would be directed to the retailer, but many queries that relate to the use of the product would be directed to the manufacturer.

We are happy to provide information regarding your transaction, but questions which relate to the product itself may be directed to the licensor of the software.

## FAQs

I require more information regarding the transaction, where can I get help?

Please reach out to our Order Support team via [help@paddle.com](mailto:help@paddle.com). Bear in mind that we are happy to provide information regarding your transaction, but questions which relate to the product itself may be directed to the licensor of the software.

I need to confirm the bank details via the phone, how can I arrange that?

Please reach out to [procurement@paddle.com](mailto:procurement@paddle.com) to make arrangements with our Order Support team.

I’m tax exempt, where do I send my exemption certificate?

Please head to [paddle.net](http://paddle.net/) and request a tax exemption, in your request include the information about what you’re purchasing and have your tax exemption certificate ready.

My company requires us to submit a purchase order prior to payment. Where can I send this?

All purchase orders should be sent directly to the software developer. If you don’t have a contact email for their sales department, submit the purchase order to the developer’s general email address found on their website. If you have trouble locating this on their website, send the developer and product name along with the website URL to [help@paddle.com](mailto:help@paddle.com) and we’ll be happy to assist you.[Paddle.com](http://paddle.com/) does not fulfill purchase orders. If you send a purchase order to [paddle.com](http://paddle.com/), we will pass this on to the developer.
